I collect personal information including name, email address, and other contact information. This data is only collected if you directly provide it to me.

I process personal information in the following ways:

- **To deliver and facilitate delivery of services to the user.** I may process your information to provide you with the requested service.
- **To respond to user inquiries/offer support to users.** I may process your information to respond to your inquiries and solve any potential issues you might have with the requested service.
- **To request feedback.** I may process your information when necessary to request feedback and to contact you about your use of the Service.
- **To identify usage trends.** I may process information about how you use the Service to better understand how they are being used so I can improve them.

I also collect non-identifying information when you visit, use, or navigate the Service. This information does not reveal your specific identity (like your name or contact information) but may include device and usage information. This information is needed to maintain the security and operation of the Service, and for internal analytics and reporting purposes.

Information I collect may include:

- Device event information (such as system activity, error reports, and hardware settings)
